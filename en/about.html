About Jan Michaelsen

I am an Executive Management Consultant with over 30 years of experience in the automotive industry. My specialty: the critical interface between corporate strategy and operational reality – particularly where procurement and sales intersect.

In 28 years at Mercedes-Benz, I gained my first leadership experience in development controlling and then took on project roles with international challenges. I held strategic functions in procurement for several decades: from powertrain systems to interiors to chassis. In parallel, I worked as a project lead and strategic controller – a combination that led me to understand both sides of the business: the big goals of the executive board and the operational realities in procurement and sales.

Since 2023, I bring this experience as an independent consultant to new projects.

Today I use all my experience as a consultant: I help companies heal the fault lines between strategy and execution – especially where procurement and sales reach their limits. My role is that of a bridge-builder who speaks and connects both worlds.

Core Competencies

  • Strategic management of the interface between procurement and sales
  • Leading complex contract and price negotiations to optimize outcomes
  • Developing sales strategies and sales excellence
  • Solution-oriented stakeholder and conflict management in supplier relationships
  • Realizing cost and efficiency improvements
  • Leading international projects in Europe, North America and Asia

My Approach

Major challenges rarely arise from one side alone – they lie in the tension between the people involved and the given circumstances. That's exactly where I start.

  • Understanding both sides: Through my experience in conflict management and the automotive industry, I develop solutions where others mainly see opposition.
  • Execution focus: Concepts are only valuable if they hold up in practice. My focus is on solutions that actually work with the people involved and under the given conditions.
  • Reliability: Lasting success is built on trust. I create stable partnerships, ensure clear accountability and develop sustainable solutions.
